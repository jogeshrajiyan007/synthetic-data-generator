Over the last few weeks, I have been experimenting with ModelMasterAI within Metaport to explore how AI can augment (not replace) traditional modeling tools like erwin.

The goal is to provide an AI copilot for data modelers that can accelerate model design and continuously improve model quality. Current capabilities include:

• AI-predicted dimensional models using a hybrid architecture (deterministic ML + SecureGPT) rather than relying entirely on LLMs. This approach minimizes token consumption, avoids SecureGPT token limitations, and produces more explainable and consistent results.

• Automated model health checks to identify issues such as missing SCD2 audit columns, PII exposure, many-to-many relationships, grain inconsistencies, and opportunities to optimize star/snowflake schemas.

• Interactive model canvas with relationship visualization, recommendations, auto-layout, versioning, and conversational model modifications.

• Inspired by the recent industry trend around Vibe Coding, I also experimented with a Vibe Data Modeling interface, powered by a multi-agent framework that can interpret natural language requests and build or modify models directly on the canvas in real time.

Interestingly, Databricks recently showcased a similar vision around "Vibe Your Data Model" at the 2026 Data + AI Summit, reinforcing that conversational and AI-assisted modeling is becoming an emerging area in the industry. Our objective is not to challenge erwin's strengths, but to complement existing modeling practices with AI-driven recommendations, continuous quality checks, and conversational model evolution.

Would be happy to walk through the prototype and discuss how this capability could evolve further within Metaport.
