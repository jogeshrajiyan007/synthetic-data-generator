Slide 4 – Architecture & Process Flow

> Before we move into the live demo, I'd like to briefly explain how we ensure AI remains trustworthy.

Every business question follows a structured reasoning process.

First, the platform understands the user's intent.

It then identifies the relevant enterprise datasets required to answer the question.

Before generating SQL, it validates the schema to ensure every table and every column actually exists.

Next comes what I believe is the most important component of the entire platform—our Semantic Layer.

This is where business language is translated into enterprise meaning.

For example, when someone asks about premium revenue, AI understands that premium information belongs to the Policy dataset.

If someone asks about customer claims, it understands how Customers, Policies and Claims relate to each other.

Finally, every request passes governance validation before execution.

Only after those validations succeed do we generate the response.

This ensures AI isn't guessing.

It's reasoning over trusted enterprise data.
