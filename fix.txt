# Oracle Integration Monitoring — Dashboard Guide

**Source:** Oracle PaaS &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Coverage:** Weekly &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Last Refresh:** Thursday, February 26, 2026

---

## 📊 What This Dashboard Tells You

This homepage gives you a **weekly snapshot** of how well HR data is being collected and processed from Oracle PaaS into the Group HR Data Factory. It surfaces data health at a glance — so you can quickly spot issues before they impact downstream reporting.

---

## 🔢 KPI Definitions

### Data Collect Insights
These metrics track the **volume and completeness** of data received this week.

| KPI | What it means |
|-----|--------------|
| **Number of Employees** | Total distinct employee records loaded this week. Trend arrow shows change vs. prior week. |
| **Files Loaded** | Number of data files successfully ingested from Oracle PaaS this week. |
| **Overall Error Rate** | Percentage of all loaded records that contain at least one data quality issue. Lower is better. |

### Data Quality Insights
These metrics measure the **quality and integrity** of the data that was loaded.

| KPI | What it means |
|-----|--------------|
| **Rows with DQ Errors** | Total number of rows flagged with at least one data quality rule violation this week. |
| **Global DQ Score** | Overall data quality score across all loaded records, expressed as a percentage. Target is 100%. |
| **Global DQ Dimension Score** | DQ score broken down by dimension — switch between **Completeness**, **Consistency**, and **Validity** to see where issues are concentrated. |

### Accepted vs. Rejected Rows
| KPI | What it means |
|-----|--------------|
| **Accepted Rows** | Records that passed all DQ checks and are ready for downstream use. |
| **Rejected Rows** | Records that failed one or more DQ rules and have been excluded from reporting until resolved. |

---

## 🧭 How to Navigate

This dashboard is designed for **progressive exploration** — start at the summary and drill into details only when needed.

- **Click "Data Collect Insights"** → Opens a detailed breakdown of file ingestion activity, employee record trends, and source-level loading stats.

- **Click "Data Quality Insights"** → Opens a granular view of DQ rule failures, including which fields, entities, or countries are driving errors.

- **Click "Investigate Errors"** → Takes you directly to a row-level error log where you can filter, sort, and identify specific records that need attention.

- **Click "Review DQ Rules"** → Opens the DQ rule registry so you can understand the logic behind each validation check and assess whether rules need updating.

---

> 💡 **Tip:** Use the **DQ Dimension toggle** (Completeness / Consistency / Validity) on the homepage to quickly identify which quality dimension needs the most attention before drilling in further.
